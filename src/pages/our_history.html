Our History

2020

It all started with a single, groundbreaking idea that struck in the quiet of the night, an idea that would turn the traditional ice cream world on its head. Our founders, Allison, Aryaa, Baydan, and Geneva found themselves questioning the status quo, pondering why ice cream flavors had to be limited to the usual suspects. This curiosity wasn’t just a passing thought; it was the kindling for a fire that would ignite a revolution in taste.

2021

In those early days, our kitchen resembled less a food prep space and more a mad scientist’s lab, filled with vials of exotic extracts and bowls brimming with unusual ingredient pairings. It was here, amidst this chaos of creativity, that our first breakthrough occurred—a flavor so unexpected, so unconventional, that it shattered all preconceived notions of what ice cream could be. Each new batch was a gamble.

2022

This phase of relentless experimentation was marked by many late nights and early mornings, fueled by a shared passion and countless cups of coffee. Our team tasted, tweaked, and sometimes, started all over again from scratch. But with each failure came a valuable lesson, and with each success, a new milestone was reached. We weren’t just making ice cream anymore; we were crafting experiences, memories, and most importantly, joy in every scoop.

2023

The path we chose was not easy. It required courage, persistence, and a sprinkle of madness. But looking back, every challenge faced and obstacle overcome only added to the richness of our journey, much like the layers of flavors in our now-celebrated ice cream creations. This is the essence of our parlor, a place where daring dreams turn into delicious reality.
